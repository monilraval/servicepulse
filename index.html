ADKAR Flow — Sequential Change Model
Fix the lowest stage first. Addressing later stages before earlier ones wastes effort.
Key principle: ADKAR is sequential. If a user is blocked at Knowledge, sending more Awareness emails wastes effort. ServicePulse diagnoses the exact blocking stage so every intervention hits the right target.

What ServicePulse Does

ServicePulse applies the ADKAR change management model to IT service adoption. Instead of tracking only whether employees have access to a tool, it diagnoses exactly which stage of the adoption journey is failing — and recommends the precise intervention needed to unblock progress.

Built to demonstrate the Adoption Lead mindset: data-driven, user-centric, and grounded in proven change management frameworks.
